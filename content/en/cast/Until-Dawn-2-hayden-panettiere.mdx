*Until Dawn 2* is designed as a standalone sequel, intentionally stepping away from the original game's characters and setting to forge its own terrifying path. This approach allows Firesprite to introduce new lore, fresh scares, and a distinct atmosphere without being constrained by the previous storyline. The move to a remote tropical island and a ghost-hunting premise offers a new canvas for the series' signature choice-based horror.

The decision to feature a new cast, rather than bringing back characters like Sam (Hayden Panettiere) or Mike, ensures that the game can deliver unexpected twists and consequences that aren't influenced by prior character arcs.

<Section icon={Target} title="Premise and Gameplay Mechanics">
**Until Dawn 2** plunges players into a fresh, terrifying narrative. The story follows a group of amateur ghost hunters, "Dead True," known for their popular paranormal YouTube channel, who have mostly faked their content. Their luck changes when they are sent to the deserted tropical island of Akashima for their first professionally funded episode. What begins as a routine job quickly escalates into a desperate fight for survival.

The island is shrouded in ancient secrets and haunted by a chilling story of a woman, revealing something far darker than the team ever anticipated. Adding to the peril, internal tensions within the group threaten to tear them apart, making their chances of survival even slimmer.

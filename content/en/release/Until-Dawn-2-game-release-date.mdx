<Section icon={Shield} title="Gameplay Mechanics and the Butterfly Effect">
True to its predecessor, **Until Dawn 2** will heavily feature the iconic **Butterfly Effect** system. This core mechanic ensures that every decision, big or small, has far-reaching consequences that shape the narrative and determine who lives and who dies. Stu Tilley, Creative Director at Firesprite, emphasized the importance of character relationships in the sequel.

"This time, how you manage relationships within the crew matters more than ever, with some branches based on where things stand between characters. It’s not just the huge moments, even small character moments can set off a Butterfly Effect, sending your story spinning off towards unforeseen consequences," Tilley stated.

  <Step num="1" title="VR-Optimized Exploration">

  <Step num="2" title="Interactive Choice System">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Environmental Interaction">
    Allow players to physically interact with objects in the environment, such as opening doors, picking up items, or examining evidence, deepening the sense of presence and discovery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Psychological Horror Elements">
    Leverage VR's unique ability to create psychological horror by playing with player perception, spatial awareness, and auditory cues in ways that are impossible on a flat screen.
  </Step>

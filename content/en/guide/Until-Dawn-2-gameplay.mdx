<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Survival Mechanics">

Survival in the sequel is significantly more challenging than in the original. With the introduction of more complex environments, players must adapt their playstyle to ensure everyone makes it through the night.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Situational Awareness">
    Listen for ambient sounds. Characters will often vocalize their concerns when a threat is nearby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Grip and Precision">
    When prompted to "grip it tight," ensure your controller input is steady. Failing these moments often leads to immediate fatal consequences.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Pathing">
    Decide between taking the safer, slower route or the faster, more dangerous path based on the character's current physical state.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Premise of Until Dawn 2">

*Until Dawn 2* introduces a brand new story and cast, focusing on a group of ghost hunters behind a popular paranormal YouTube channel called *Dead True*. Their content has always been staged, but their first professionally funded episode on the deserted tropical island of Akishima changes everything. What begins as a routine job quickly devolves into a fight for survival against ancient secrets and a chilling, vengeful entity.

The game's narrative will heavily rely on the "Butterfly Effect" system, where choices and consequences ripple through the story, impacting character relationships and survival outcomes. Stu Tilley, Creative Director of Firesprite, emphasized that "how you manage relationships within the crew matters more than ever," with even small interactions potentially triggering unforeseen consequences.

<Callout type="tip">

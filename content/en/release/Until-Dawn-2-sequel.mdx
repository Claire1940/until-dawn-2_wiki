<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay: The Evolution of Choice and Consequence">
True to its roots, the **Until Dawn 2 sequel** will heavily feature the "Butterfly Effect" system, where every decision, big or small, can have unforeseen and dramatic consequences. This time, however, the system is enhanced to place a greater emphasis on character relationships within the crew. Managing these dynamics will be crucial, as the standing between characters can open or close specific narrative branches.

<Callout type="info" title="Relationship Dynamics">
Creative Director Stu Tilley highlighted that "how you manage relationships within the crew matters more than ever, with some branches based on where things stand between characters." This suggests a deeper and more intricate social system influencing the plot.
</Callout>

Players will face gut-wrenching decisions, such as risking injury to save wildlife, maintaining a scandalous affair, or pushing a crew member to their breaking point for the sake of content. These choices will determine who lives, who dies, and ultimately, the fate of the "Dead True" crew. The game promises a modern horror experience packed with intrigue, emotion, and twists that will keep players on edge.

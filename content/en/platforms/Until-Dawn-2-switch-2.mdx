<Section icon={Shield} title="Gameplay Mechanics: Butterfly Effect and Relationships">
At its core, *Until Dawn 2* will leverage the iconic "Butterfly Effect" system, a hallmark of the series. Every decision, big or small, will ripple through the narrative, leading to unforeseen consequences and shaping the fate of the characters. This branching narrative structure is designed to offer high replayability, encouraging players to explore different choices and outcomes.

Stu Tilley, Creative Director of Firesprite, emphasized the enhanced importance of character relationships in the sequel. "This time, how you manage relationships within the crew matters more than ever, with some branches based on where things stand between characters. It’s not just the huge moments, even small character moments can set off a Butterfly Effect, sending your story spinning off towards unforeseen consequences…"

<Callout type="success" title="Enhanced Choice & Consequence">
The deepened relationship mechanics mean that player choices will not only impact individual survival but also the group's cohesion and dynamic, adding another layer of complexity to the horror.
</Callout>

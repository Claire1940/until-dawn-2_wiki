<Section icon={Swords} title="The Butterfly Effect: Decisions and Consequences">
A cornerstone of the *Until Dawn* experience is the "Butterfly Effect" mechanic, and the *Until Dawn 2 trailer* confirms its return. This system emphasizes that even the smallest choices can lead to drastic, unforeseen outcomes, shaping the fate of the characters. The trailer highlights a particularly harrowing example: the choice to push a friend off a cliff to potentially save them, or to jump without them. This kind of brutal decision-making is at the heart of the game.

The original *Until Dawn* was praised for its branching narratives, offering immense replayability as players explored different paths and outcomes. *Until Dawn 2* appears to amplify this, promising even more complex and morally ambiguous choices that will keep players on the edge of their seats.

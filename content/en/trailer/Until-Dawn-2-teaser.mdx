<Section icon={Map} title="A Brand New Haunting: Akashima Island's Secrets">
**Until Dawn 2** transports players to Akashima Island, a seemingly idyllic tropical paradise with a sinister past. This new setting is far from the isolated mountain lodge of the original, offering fresh opportunities for exploration and terror. The island harbors centuries-old secrets, hungry for vengeance, and our new cast finds themselves right in the middle of it.

The protagonists are a crew of ghost hunters from the popular paranormal channel "Dead True." Ironically, their on-screen scares have always been staged, as they've never encountered anything truly supernatural. All that changes on Akashima Island, forcing them to confront genuine horrors and live up to their fabricated reputation to survive until dawn.

<Section icon={Target} title="Choices and Consequences: The Butterfly Effect Returns">
True to the **Until Dawn** legacy, player choices are at the core of the gameplay experience in **Until Dawn 2**. The renowned Butterfly Effect system makes a grand return, ensuring that every decision, big or small, can lead to unforeseen consequences, drastically altering the narrative and determining who lives and who dies.

This time, managing relationships within the ghost-hunting crew is more critical than ever. The complicated dynamics between characters will influence branching storylines. Will you prioritize a scandalous affair, risk injury to save an animal, or push a crewmate to their breaking point for the sake of viral content? These choices will not only shape individual fates but the entire group's chances of survival.

| Choice Type       | Example Scenario                                        | Potential Consequence                                  |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Moral Dilemma** | Save a cute baby boar from a trap vs. avoid danger.     | Character injury, or moral boost/guilt.                |
| **Relationship**  | Keep a secret affair vs. expose it to the group.        | Group cohesion, trust issues, betrayal.                |
| **Survival Priority** | Focus on content for boss vs. immediate escape.         | Increased danger, character death, lost opportunity.    |
| **Intervention**  | Help a struggling teammate vs. prioritize self-preservation. | Stronger bonds, or resentment and isolation.            |

<Callout type="success" title="Mastering the Butterfly Effect">
